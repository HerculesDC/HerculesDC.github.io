HErC's First p5 post

Moon Phases, with names

This is a simple project I used to try p5.js. Even though p5 has 3D capabilities, this project was made entirely in 2D, due to platform limitations where I originally coded it. I tried to give it a 3D-ish feel using a couple of the platform's different features such as transparency. The Moon is not an actual circle: it's a combination of four Bezier curves, with their control points spaced to best approximate a circle. This setup also allowed me to manipulate each Bezier vertex to draw a crescent according to the moon phase. The transparency also helps draw a more-or-less convincing terminator, simulating 3D illumination.
